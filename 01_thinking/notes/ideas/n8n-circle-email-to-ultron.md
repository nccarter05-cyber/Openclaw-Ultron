# N8N Workflow: Circle Email → Ultron

## Idea
Create an N8N workflow that automatically forwards emails received on circle email to Ultron.

## Trigger
- Incoming email on circle email address

## Action
- Forward/relay email content to Ultron

## Benefit
- Automatic routing of circle emails to Ultron for processing
- No manual forwarding needed
- Ultron can handle email classification, response drafting, or archiving
