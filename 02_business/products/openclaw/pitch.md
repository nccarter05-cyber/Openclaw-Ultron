## The One-Liner

"Your team is handling repetitive tasks that machines should do."

- Sales reps doing manual prospecting instead of selling
- Support team drowning in routine questions
- Appointment scheduling eating 10+ hours per week

"These tasks are:

## The Pitch Flow

### Meeting Opener
"We work with [industry] companies that are trying to scale without adding headcount. Usually the bottleneck is repetitive tasks—prospecting, scheduling, support questions. We've built a platform that automates those with AI agents. Not chatbots. Actual agents that integrate with your systems. Does that sound like something worth 20 minutes?"

### Discovery
- What tasks are taking the most time?
- How many people handle this today?
- What's the cost of not automating?
- Which systems do you use? (CRM, phone, etc.)

### The Demo
Show:
1. **Agent behavior** — How it handles a real task (not a generic demo)
2. **Integration** — Pulling data from their CRM, updating records
3. **Monitoring** — Real-time dashboard of agent activity
4. **Cost** — Pricing for their specific use case

### Close
"If we can reduce your support team from 5 to 3 people, that's $200k/year saved. The system costs $5k/month. Payback in less than 4 weeks. Want to run a 30-day pilot?"

## Objections & Responses

### "We've tried chatbots, they're terrible"
"Most chatbots are just pattern matching. OpenClaw agents actually understand context, integrate with your systems, and make real decisions. Plus they're trained on your specific business logic, not generic AI."

### "This seems too good to be true"
"Most ROI comes from automation + integration. You could buy Zapier and cobble things together, but it takes months. Or hire developers for 6 months. OpenClaw is the middle ground—fast, affordable, integrated."

### "What if the AI makes a bad decision?"
"Everything is logged. You set rules—if the agent isn't confident, it escalates to a human. You maintain full control. This isn't 'set and forget,' it's 'monitor and improve.'"

### "Your competitor costs less"
"They probably do. But are they integrating with your CRM? Handling phone calls? Providing monitoring? Compare feature-for-feature."
